About Us

This PPE Donation Portal is part of an initiative by Irish Rail to distribute surplus Personal Protective Equipment to people in need. By making a small donation, you're not only receiving high-quality safety gear but also contributing to local community welfare.

Our Mission

We aim to make surplus PPE gear accessible to low-income individuals and communities through a simplified donation-based platform.
